Welcome to Civil Department of Engineering

Vision of Department

To enhance Civil Engineering discipline that will help to the students to contribute towards the growth of rural areas, industries, society and in nation development

Mission of Department

M1:To impart high quality based technical education to the students of civil engineering.
M2:To serve the construction industry, civil engineering career and rural network via dissemination of expertise and technical services
M3:To create awareness among civil engineering students about environmental and societal issues.
M4:to inculcate a spirit of life-long learning with ethical bent of thoughts for better career enhancement
